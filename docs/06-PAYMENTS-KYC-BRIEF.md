# CodeSlot — Business Description for Payment Processors (KYC / Onboarding)

Reusable description for Razorpay appeals, Cashfree, or a Merchant-of-Record
(Lemon Squeezy / Polar / Dodo Payments) application. The goal is to be
categorized correctly: **B2B SaaS / Digital Advertising Services**, *not*
"Pay Per Click" or a payout/affiliate network.

## What CodeSlot is
CodeSlot is a B2B software-as-a-service advertising platform. Businesses
(advertisers) purchase advertising campaigns to display a single, unobtrusive
sponsored message inside a developer tool (a Visual Studio Code extension).

## What the payment processor actually processes
**Only inbound payments from business advertisers.** Advertisers prepay into an
advertising wallet (a digital balance) and allocate it to campaigns. Billing is
**campaign-based and prepaid** (CPM / prepaid budget), not a click-payout scheme.

## What we do NOT do (important)
- **No cash payouts to individuals or consumers** through the payment processor
  or by any other means.
- Developers who use the free extension are rewarded with **in-application
  credits** that can be redeemed **only** for third-party AI API usage (digital
  service / API tokens) — never money, cash, gift cards, or bank transfers.
- We are **not** an affiliate network, a "pay per click" payout network, a
  wallet/PPI, or a money-transfer service.

## Correct merchant category
SaaS / Software / Digital Services / Digital Advertising Services (B2B).

## Compliance & site readiness
The website provides all standard policy pages, publicly linked in the footer:
- Terms & Acceptable Use — `/terms`
- Privacy Policy — `/privacy`
- Refund & Cancellation Policy — `/refund`
- Contact Us (registered business name, address, email, phone) — `/contact`

Advertiser accounts require authenticated sign-in (Google / GitHub / email) and
agree to the Acceptable Use Policy, which prohibits adult, gambling, malware,
phishing, and other restricted content. Every campaign is auto-screened before
it can serve.

## One-line summary
"A B2B SaaS platform where businesses prepay for software-developer advertising
campaigns; end users receive non-cash in-app credits redeemable only for
third-party AI API usage."
